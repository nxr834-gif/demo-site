The short story of Sam the Hobbit

Sam Tripp looks like a hobbit that never found a way out of its hole. He is from a big hole.
The biggest hole out of any other hobbit. During his time in his hole he
enjoys eating goat cheese from his indoor pet goat named jerry, and he also enjoys
smoking grass in his brass pipe.

Lifestyle of Sam the hobbit

Sam's daily routine consists of physical labor.
He cares for his plants, cleans the pathway that stretches from the front of his
lawn to the front of his doorstep, and he gives his goat a bath.
